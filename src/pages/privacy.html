What personal data we collect and why

Media

Visitors of the website can download and extract any location data from the images on the website. Thus, if you upload images to the website, try to avoid uploading images with embedded location data (EXIF GPS)

Comments

When visitors leave comments on the site they automatically permit us to collect the data in the comments form. We gather visitor’s IP address and browser user agent to help spam detection.

We use Gravatar service to see if you use the anonymized string created from your email address (also called a hash). After the approval of your comment, your profile picture will be seen to the public in the context of your comment.

Contact form

Cookies

You can refuse to leave your data again when you write a comment. If you leave a comment on our site you may refuse to save your name, email address and website in cookies. The cookies are stored for one year.

If you are logged in to this site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. These cookies do not contain your personal data, moreover, all the information about you disappears when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies usually last for two days, when screen options cookies last for a year. If you click on the “remember me” button, your login will be saved for two weeks. If you log out of your account, login cookies will be deleted.

When you write or edit your comment text, there will save additional cookies in your browser. It doesn’t contain personal data; it just indicates the post ID of the text or comment you just edited. It expires after 1 day.

Content embedded from other websites

This website may include different types of content (e.g. articles, images, videos, records, etc.) which are embedded from other websites. The content from other websites behaves in the same way, just like the user has visited the other website.

The websites may collect data about your interaction with other websites and monitor the interaction with embedded content, in case if you have an account and are logged to the website.

Who we share your data with, and how long we save your data?

When you leave your comment, its metadata and the text are retained indefinitely. This is done so that we can automatically recognize and approve any subsequent comments, and not keep them in the queue for moderation.

We store personal information of users who register on our website. We save only the information they provide in their user profile. All users can edit, delete, share their personal information at any time they want. The one thing they can’t change is their username. Website admins also can see and edit the information.

What rights you have over your data?

You can request to receive a file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us when you have an account on this site and left comments. You can also apply to us so we could delete any personal data we hold about you. We don't store any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where we send your data

Your comments may be checked using the automatic spam detection service.

How we protect your data?

You have the right to control the use of your personal information because your personal data must be esteemed. We strictly limit the collection and processing of your personal information and to the best of our abilities. We work only with other organizations who do the same. We don’t use the personal data you provide to us for private purposes.

What data violation procedures we have in place?

  • We are able to recognize a personal data disturbance.
  • We understand that personal data violation includes not only a loss but also a theft of personal data.
  • We have a special plan for addressing only any personal data breaches that can happen.
  • There are special team managers engaged in violation management.
  • Our managers are able to define the breach and resolve the situation as soon as possible.
